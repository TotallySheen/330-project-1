Game of Life Playground

Click to Draw or Kill Cells

Description

This is a visualization of Conway's Game of Life, with various tools in place to experiment with changing of the game's core rules. These tools include:

  • Changing the number of neighbors that cause life or death.
  • Some common presets to mess around with.
  • Click functionality to paint your own patterns.

Enjoy!
